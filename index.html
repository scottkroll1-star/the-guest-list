Operator Manual

1. Add Guests: Enter the names of the guests, experts, or personalities you wish to monitor. You can track as many as you need.

2. Engage Spam Shield: This is your primary defense against YouTube's algorithm.
• Low: For niche or academic experts. Minimal filtering.
• Medium: The default. Blocks standard reaction and drama clips.
• High: Total lockdown. Forces the guest's name to be in the video title, blocking passing mentions, foreign translations, AI summaries, and grifter networks.

3. Initialize Feed: Tap to compile your targets into a clean, unified launch pad.

4. The Dashboard: Tap any Guest Card to instantly launch a perfectly filtered, long-form YouTube query. When the video ends, simply swipe back to return to your dashboard and select your next guest.
