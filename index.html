The Guest List

The most efficient way to monitor multiple guests at once.

Operator Manual

1. Add Guests: Enter the names of the experts or personalities you wish to monitor.

2. Engage Shield: Your defense against the algorithm.
• Lo: For niche experts. Minimal filtering.
• Med: Default. Blocks standard reaction clips.
• Hi: Total lockdown. Forces the guest's name to be in the video title, blocking passing mentions, foreign translations, AI summaries, and grifter networks.

3. The Dashboard: Tap any Guest Card to launch a perfectly filtered YouTube query. Swipe back when finished to select the next guest.
