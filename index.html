Operator Manual

Tracking nomadic experts who bounce around the long-form ecosystem.
1. How to Use

Add Guests: Enter the names of the experts or personalities you wish to monitor. You can track as many guests as necessary.

Primary Controls: Select your desired timeframe and Spam Shield setting.
• Low: Minimal filtering.
• Medium & High: Both settings aggressively block reaction clips, summaries, and foreign translations. High mode additionally forces the guest's name to be in the video title.

Advanced Settings: Use this menu to refine your search parameters. Whitelist specific topic keywords, or blacklist YouTube channels.

The Dashboard: Tap "Initialize Feed" to compile your list into a clean launch pad. Tap any card to launch a strictly filtered query. Swipe back when finished to return to your dashboard.

2. FAQ & Known Quirks

Q: Why am I seeing old videos when I search "Today"?
A: When a search yields exactly zero recent results, YouTube reacts in one of two ways. Occasionally, it will surrender and show a clean "No Results Found" page. More often, the algorithm refuses to show a blank page and instantly refills your screen with historical or popular videos to keep you clicking. If you search "Today" and the first video is weeks old, ignore the noise; it simply means your guest has no new appearances.
